  Project Overview

This project provides a unified analytical view of:

- Economic performance (GDP growth, inflation)  
- Credit dynamics (credit growth and risk exposure)  
- Composite risk scoring  
- Expansion capacity estimation  
- Portfolio allocation across multiple countries  
- Stress testing under adverse economic scenarios  

The core objective is to bridge the gap between macroeconomic conditions and credit decision-making, enabling institutions to evaluate risk, simulate scenarios, and optimize capital deployment through a single analytical interface.

  Key Objectives

- Translate risk metrics into practical expansion strategies (Defensive / Moderate / Aggressive)  
- Support multi-market portfolio optimization based on risk-adjusted returns  
- Perform economic stress testing to estimate potential credit losses  
- Provide executive-level summaries to assist strategic decision-making

  Key Decision Insights

The platform supports decision-making across three core areas:

 1. Overall Market Assessment  
Integrated evaluation using GDP growth, inflation, and credit growth to assess economic health and risk.

 2. Defining a Safe Expansion Range  
Estimates expansion size based on partial capital allocation (not full deployment), enabling balanced growth while preserving capital.

 3. Intelligent Capital Allocation

- Reduce overall exposure  
- Improve risk-adjusted returns  
- Enhance diversification
